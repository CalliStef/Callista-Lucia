# Creating & Using Frames
{: .no_toc }

Frames are one of the most foundational elements of Figma. They can serve as the container or viewport holding your app’s design components, but can also be a component in your design itself. In the coming section, we will be creating our first frame on Figma and getting our workspace ready to work with.

## Task 1 - Creating Your First Frame on Figma
**1.1** To start your new design project, _click_ on the “New design file” button inside the **Dashboard**.

**1.2** Inside of your new work area, you will be presented with a **Navigation Menu** containing eight icons. _Click_ on the grid-like icon (representing a **Frame**) highlighted in blue below. 

<div align="center">
